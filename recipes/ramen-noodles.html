Ramen Noodles

Description

Ramen noodles are a popular Japanese dish that consists of wheat noodles served in a meat or fish-based broth. The noodles are typically topped with sliced pork, dried seaweed, green onions, and a soft-boiled egg. Ramen is a comforting and satisfying meal that is perfect for a cold day or when you're feeling under the weather. This recipe is a basic ramen noodle recipe that is easy to make and can be customized with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 2 packs of ramen noodles
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon miso paste
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup sliced green onions
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels
  • 1/2 cup sliced bamboo shoots
  • 1/2 cup sliced pork or chicken
  • 2 soft-boiled eggs

Steps

  1. In a large pot, bring chicken broth and water to a boil.
  2. Add soy sauce, miso paste, sesame oil, rice vinegar, ginger, and garlic to the pot.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Cook ramen noodles according to package instructions.
  5. Divide cooked noodles between two bowls.
  6. Top noodles with broth and desired toppings.
  7. Serve ramen hot and enjoy!
